Defining The Problem

Echipamentul.ro - is a online shop that sells safety equipment. They offer a wide variety of merchandise. Because of the cooling of the pandemic their store registered an increase in clients and they decided that a rebrand was necessary.

Services offered



BRANDING   UI/UX   WEBDESIGN   SOCIAL MEDIA TEMPLATES   PRODUCT PHOTOGRAPHY


Research



The client wanted a new identity, something to represent safety, security, help and overall dependable

We ENTERED INTO THE RESEARCH AREA WITH OUR MINDS OPEN TO BE ABLE TO Offer something that represents their vision and identity.

Proposal



For the logo We went into the direction of a fireman because of their ability to help when in need and also because of the fact that they always put safety first.


We started working at the rebranding process offering a new visual identity and all the assets necessary to be able to deliver to our client exactly what they wanted.
